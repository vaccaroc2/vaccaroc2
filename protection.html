How to Protect Yourself on Public Wi-Fi

  • Use a VPN to encrypt your internet connection.
  • Always check for HTTPS in website URLs.
  • Disable auto-connect and file sharing features.
  • Enable a firewall on your device.
  • Use two-factor authentication (2FA) for online accounts.
  • Install and update antivirus software regularly.

Helpful Tools:

  • VPN apps with malware and ad-blocking features
  • Secure browsers like Mozilla Firefox and Brave
  • Encrypted messaging apps and password managers

Looking Ahead:

New technologies like AI are being used to improve public Wi-Fi security. It is crucial to stay informed and practice good cybersecurity habits to stay safe in an increasingly connected world.
